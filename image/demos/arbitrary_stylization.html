mi.ArbitraryStyleTransferNetwork

ArbitraryStyleTransferNetwork wraps around Reiichiro Nakano's TensorFlow.js port of Ghiasi et al.'s arbitrary stylization model. The demo below performs stylization on a content/style image pair by

  • calling the predictStyleParameters method with the style image to obtain style-specific "bottleneck" parameters, and
  • calling the stylize method with the content image and the bottleneck parameters to perform stylization.

It then displays the result on a canvas via TensorFlow.js' toPixels function.
