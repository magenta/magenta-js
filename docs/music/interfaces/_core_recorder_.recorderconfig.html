Interface RecorderConfig

An interface for providing configurable properties to a Recorder.

param

The tempo at which to play the click track.

param

Whether to play a click track while recording.

param

Whether to play a count-in click at the beginning of the recording.

param

Whether to start the note time offset at the first note received instead of the start of the recording. Defaults to false.
